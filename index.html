About me

When I was a student (13 years old), I tasted my first job experience in jewelry workings for summers. I did this till 17. In this process, I've learned about "how do we build things" and "what things we should care about in the building process." Then I tried working in cafes as a waiter and barista. there I learned a lot about "soft skills." After a while, I discovered that I was not using my full potential, so I started searching for my ideal job. I tried Carpentry, Different theater performance sections, Jewelry Design, and more during this period. Finally, I found myself interested in Design with this definition: "The art of solving problems." I saw a Design theme in every experience I've tried, which was like finding a missing puzzle piece for me. UI/UX was a good area for me to start for many reasons. For example, my educational background in software engineering (associate degree). I know the basics of development; so I can build good communication with developers =) Now, after working on eight projects and two years of focusing on the UI/UX area, I'm looking forward to finding a place where I can Improve myself more by helping the company in their way.

UI/UX Designer
